I’ve tried for many years to attain a job in programming but without success. Among the ways that I’ve taught myself are through textbooks, online course specializations, Kata on Codewars, and working through hundreds of algorithms in interview textbooks. I ran into a problem when I applied for interviews. There’s a lot of things in computer science I’ve never heard of, and I often felt like I was in over my head. Why I Love Programming

For me programming is the perfect mix of left and right brain skills. I love math and I come from an engineering background, and I’m also a writer and a musician. Programming is a wonderful fusion between the right and left brain and a tremendously creative outlet. The more constraints there are in programming, the more creativity it seems to demand. When I’m programming I feel like a child playing a fun new game. I really want to spend the rest of my life this way.
